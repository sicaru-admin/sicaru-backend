---

## Content Pillars

All content should fall into one of these four pillars:

1. **Educación** (40%) — Hair care tips, guides, how-tos, product education
2. **Producto** (30%) — Product demos, comparisons, unboxings, new arrivals
3. **Comunidad** (20%) — Customer stories, salon features, events, reviews
4. **Promoción** (10%) — Sales, discounts, offers, bundles

This ratio keeps the audience engaged without feeling overly promotional.
