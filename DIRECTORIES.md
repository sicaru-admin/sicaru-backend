## Tier 1 — Critical (Do First Week)

These directories have the highest impact on local search, AI discoverability, and customer trust. Complete all five within the first week of launch.

### 1. Google Business Profile

- **URL:** https://business.google.com
- **Why:** #1 local search ranking factor. Powers Google Maps, Google Search local pack, and Google Shopping local inventory.
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://business.google.com and sign in with the Sicarú Google account.
  2. Click **Add your business to Google**.
  3. Enter business name: `Distribuidora Sicarú`.
  4. Select category: **Beauty Supply Store** (`Tienda de productos de belleza`).
  5. Add the address exactly as shown in the NAP block above.
  6. Add phone number: `+52 828 111 1023`.
  7. Add website: `https://www.distribuidorasicaru.com`.
  8. Add hours of operation.
  9. Add business description (copy from above).
  10. Upload at least 5 photos: storefront, interior, products, logo, team.
  11. **Verify:** Google will send a postcard to the address (takes 5–14 days) or offer phone/email verification. Complete verification ASAP.
- **Post-verification:**
  - Add products to the Google Business Profile product catalog.
  - Enable messaging.
  - Post weekly updates (new products, promotions).
  - Respond to all reviews within 24 hours.

### 2. Foursquare

- **URL:** https://foursquare.com/add-place
- **Why:** Foursquare's data feeds ChatGPT, Apple Maps, Uber, Samsung, and 150,000+ apps. This is how AI assistants find your business.
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://foursquare.com/add-place.
  2. Sign up or log in.
  3. Enter business name: `Distribuidora Sicarú`.
  4. Set category: **Beauty Supply Store**.
  5. Enter the full address from the NAP block.
  6. Add phone number and website.
  7. Submit the listing.
  8. **Claim the listing:** Go to https://business.foursquare.com, search for the listing, and click **Claim**.
  9. Complete verification (usually email or phone).
- **Important:** Foursquare is ChatGPT's primary source for local business data. A verified Foursquare listing significantly increases your chances of being recommended by AI assistants.

### 3. Facebook Business Page

- **URL:** https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
- **Why:** Second-largest local search platform in Mexico. Facebook Shops integration. Customer reviews and messaging.
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/pages/create.
  2. Select **Business or Brand**.
  3. Page name: `Distribuidora Sicarú`.
  4. Category: **Beauty Supply Store**.
  5. Add the full address, phone, website, and hours.
  6. Add description (copy from above).
  7. Upload profile photo (logo) and cover photo (storefront or product display).
  8. **Enable Facebook Shop** (if applicable) to display products.
  9. Set up automated messaging responses.
  10. Link to Instagram (see next step).

### 4. Instagram Business Profile

- **URL:** https://www.instagram.com (switch to Business in settings)
- **Why:** Primary visual discovery platform for beauty products in Mexico. Reels and Stories drive engagement.
- **Steps:**
  1. Create or convert an existing Instagram account to a **Business Profile**.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account → Business.
  3. Link to the Facebook Business Page.
  4. Username: `@distribuidorasicaru` (or closest available).
  5. Add full contact info: phone, email, address.
  6. Add website link: `https://www.distribuidorasicaru.com`.
  7. Bio example:
     ```
     Cuidado capilar profesional en Cadereyta, NL
     Küül • Voglia • Nekane • Hidra Color • Montis
     Envíos mismo día | Mayoreo para salones
     Compra por WhatsApp
     ```
  8. Set up Instagram Shopping (requires Facebook Shop catalog).

### 5. Apple Business Connect

- **URL:** https://businessconnect.apple.com
- **Why:** Powers Apple Maps, Siri suggestions, Spotlight search, and Safari. Critical for iPhone users (60%+ of Mexican smartphone market in target demographics).
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to https://businessconnect.apple.com.
  2. Sign in with an Apple ID.
  3. Search for `Distribuidora Sicarú` or click **Add New Business**.
  4. Fill in: name, category (Beauty Supply Store), address, phone, website, hours.
  5. Add logo and photos.
  6. **Verify ownership** — Apple may call the listed phone number.
  7. Once verified, add action links (order online, message on WhatsApp).

## Tips for Maximum Impact

### Consistency is Everything
- Use the **exact same NAP** on every directory. Even small differences ("Local 204" vs "Loc. 204" vs "L-204") confuse search engines and AI systems.
- Use the same business description (or minor variations) everywhere.
- Use the same logo and photos across all platforms.

### Photos to Prepare (upload to all directories)
1. **Storefront exterior** — La Plaza entrance with visible signage
2. **Interior** — product shelves, organized displays
3. **Product close-ups** — Küül, Voglia, Nekane, etc.
4. **Logo** — high-resolution, square format (1:1)
5. **Team** — owner/staff (builds trust)
6. **Cover photo** — wide format (16:9) showing the store

### Review Strategy
- After completing each transaction, ask the customer to leave a review on Google.
- Send a WhatsApp follow-up 3 days after delivery with a direct Google review link.
- Target: 10 Google reviews in the first month, 25 by month 3.
- Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google's policies).
